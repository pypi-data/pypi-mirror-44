Overview

snorkel is a time based data analysis UI that is complementary to your monitoring software. After you spot an anomaly in a time series graph, snorkel helps you create, refine and compare queries on your data that explain why your graphs are changing.

take a tour read the guide get started

Example Uses

  • monitor push and deployment health
  • track and diagnose client errors
  • diagnose performance regressions
  • locate optimization opportunities
  • analyze performance experiments

Features

  • Tables, Time Series, Histograms and Raw Sample Views
  • Realtime drilldowns
  • Powerful comparison queries
  • Dashboarding with Grafana

Built With

  • python, backbone and underscore
  • sybil datastore
  • twitter bootstrap
  • charting with nvd3
